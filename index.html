About Us

Sattva Green Hub unites organic producers, sustainable artisans, and eco-conscious buyers. We enable fair trade, digital empowerment, and a transparent platform for Kerala’s green creators.

Our Vision

To make Kerala a global model for sustainable living by empowering local farmers and artisans through technology and fair trade practices.
